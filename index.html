Property Management

Knowledge
We have sound knowledge of the complexities of the Residential Tenancy Act.

Rent
Rent paid are transferred to owners on a monthly/fortnightly basis along with a detailed financial statement.

Rent collection
Credo has zero tolerance for late or non-payment of rent. In an event of a default, a robust and proven process is implemented to ensure everything is back on track fast.

Rent reviews
Regular market research on rent/tenancy trends is a large part of what we do. Your property will always be returning the highest possible rent, in line with current market values.

Initial inspection
An initial inspection is carried out prior to commencement of the tenancy. Specific details on the overall condition of the property are recorded along with a gallery of photographs. The end result is a comprehensive report which forms the basis of all future property inspections.

Inspections
Inspections are carried out four times a year as a minimum during each tenancy to ensure your investment is being taken care of.

Maintenance
To ensure your investment is maintained, we can arrange essential/urgent repairs and can obtain two quotes for larger ticket items. Along with looking after the maintenance side of your investment, we also provide suggestions which will secure the longevity of your investment.

Selecting and vetting the right tenants
We will conduct viewings of the property to potential tenants. If the tenant is suitable, we will complete a rigorous background check which includes credit and in-depth reference checks.

Signing up a tenant
We will sign on your behalf a tenancy agreement with the new tenants, the agreement is supported with the initial property report and any previous damage or specific rules you wish to have included.
The bond paid is collected and lodged with the Ministry of Housing Bond Centre.

Management Fee
At Credo, we have a simple, easy to understand fee structure with no additional costs or surprises. We thrive on delivering quality service that is responsive, effective and affordable.
